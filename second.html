History of the Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower, or as the locals call it "The Iron Lady", was built for the World's Fair in Paris in 1889. The construction took two years. It was first called "300-meter tower".
Nowadays it is the most famous landmark in Paris, France.

Basic informations

Height : 324 meters Situation : Paris, France Architecte : Gustave Eiffel
